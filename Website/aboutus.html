About Us

Future Hope - Education

Future Hope Education was founded in January 2017. We mainly offer English for professional, academic and communicative purposes. We also provide standardized as well as customized corporate training programmes to organizations from different industries. The major teaching approach we pursue is communicative and student-oriented approach. We guarantee that students of the SPACE Language Academy achieve English language skills that can worthily reflect the level of each course.

Our Vision

To lead the way in offering the high-quality education for our students to achieve academic and communicative competence aligned with their intellectual, linguistic and professional growth.

Our Mission

To promote and advance an international and intellectual dimension that can encourage learners to pursue professional, academic and communicative English in a learning-facilitated atmosphere guided by proficient faculty members with top-notch facilities.

What is future hope education ?

Students will cover the material with study tools adapted to capabilities of a student. As a result, students will be challenged with harder tasks and questions when a certain level is achieved. Those who experience difficulties with a subject will get the chance to practice more until they reach the required level.
